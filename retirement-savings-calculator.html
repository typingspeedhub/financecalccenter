How this retirement calculator works

This calculator estimates how your retirement savings may grow based on regular monthly contributions and an assumed annual return. It also adjusts the final value for inflation to show your real purchasing power.

For long-term planning, compounding and inflation are critical. Even small differences in return or savings rate can lead to large changes over decades.
