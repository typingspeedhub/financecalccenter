How to Pay Off Debt Faster (Snowball vs Avalanche)

Two proven strategies to become debt-free faster — with clear USD examples and practical tips.

Run your own numbers

Try the Debt Payoff Calculator to see payoff time and interest savings.

Paying off debt feels overwhelming for many people. The key is choosing a strategy you can stick with. Two of the most popular approaches are the Debt Snowball and Debt Avalanche.

Debt Snowball (Motivation First)

Pay off the smallest balance first to build momentum and motivation.

Debt Avalanche (Math First)

Focus on the highest interest rate first to minimize total interest paid.

Which One Is Better?

Snowball is best for motivation. Avalanche is best for saving money. The best method is the one you can follow consistently.
