How this savings goal calculator works

This tool calculates the monthly amount you need to save to reach a target balance by a certain time, using:

  • Your savings goal amount
  • Your current savings
  • The number of years you want to save
  • An expected annual interest rate (optional)

It assumes monthly contributions and monthly compounding. If the result shows that your current savings already meet or exceed your goal, the required monthly saving will be shown as zero.

Use this calculator to plan an emergency fund, a down payment, a big purchase or any other medium- to long-term savings goal.
